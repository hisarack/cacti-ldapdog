Introduction

LDAPDOG is Cacti's plugin which let your Cacti supports multi-tenancy funcionality by OpenLDAP. For example, your company has 2 teams who are responsible to maintain full system. Team 1 is responisble for networking. Team 2 is responsible for system and database. They use Cacti to monitor system's status. LDAPDOG can let each team only operates their server's monitoing. Team 1 cannot modify the monitoring configuration which is belonged to Team 2. The example of OpenLDAP diagram is shown as below:

Installation

  1. Notice! It's only work in Cacti 0.8.8b now.
  2. Configure Cacti LDAP authentication setting.
  3. Set LDAP User Template, change default policy of tree permissions from allow to deny.
  4. Replace auth_login.php in Cacti Folder, and put plugins/ldapdog into plugins folder.
  5. Enable LDAPDOG plugin. Well done!
